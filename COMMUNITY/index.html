Our Mission

The Re-Store Warehouse is itself a non-profit, founded in 1999 as a 501(c)(3) from a grant provided by Cumberland Community Foundation and governed by a board of directors. Our mission is to support local charities that help the less fortunate in Fayetteville and surrounding areas.
